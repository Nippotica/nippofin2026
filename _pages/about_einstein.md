## Daijiro Nishi 

[Daijiro Nishi](https://www.linkedin.com/in/daijiro-nishi/) spent decades trading interest rate derivatives at JPMorgan, Deutsche Bank, Société Générale, and SMBC Nikko Securities, where he ran FX and IR derivatives desks. He knows how institutional finance works—and where it breaks. 

Now as CEO of Nippotica Corporation and Senior Director at University of Tokyo's Development Office, he bridges Japanese institutional markets with cutting-edge technology, applying decades of trading discipline to building companies that deliver solutions at scale.
